Once in a lifetime

A perfect trip to Mexico

In summer of 2016 me and my wife decided to go to Mexico for a one in a lifetime experience.
We decided to take a whole month off so that we could have enough time to visit multiple states.
Part of me always wanted to visit multiple parts of Mexico. Mexico is such a big country that it's
almost impossible to know the whole country no matter how much you travel.



Cascadas de Agua Azul

Our first stop in the trip was to the Cascadas of Agua Azul "Blue Waterfalls" These beautiful waterfalls span
for almost 7 miles. It's considered one of the 10 natural wonders of the world.

San Cristobal de las Casas

Next on my list is the magical town of San Cristobal de las casas. This magical town was
originally a Spanish Settlement about 300 years ago. But now it has become a popular
international destination. This place mixes the magical feeling of an old town with the
culinary influences of countries all over the world.


On San Cristobal de las Casas there are dozens of artisanal restaurants which cater to every
possible food destination in the world. Migrants from all over the world have settled in this town
situated roughly 4,000 feet up in the mountains.
